## Backstory: *“She Who Answered Before She Asked”*

The Caller was not always called that.

She was once a scholar of structured magic—someone who believed that knowledge could be contained, categorized, and mastered. But in studying forbidden arcane systems tied to Apocrypha-like structures, she made a discovery that unraveled that belief entirely.

Some knowledge does not wait to be learned.

It responds.

Her earliest experiments involved attempting to retrieve lost magical texts from impossible sources. At first, the results were fragmented. Then inconsistent. Then intelligent.

Eventually, they began answering back without prompting.

The College of Winterhold expelled her after multiple containment failures, but by then it was already too late. Her mind no longer distinguished between thought and response, between question and intrusion.

Now she does not cast spells in the traditional sense.

She asks.

And something answers.

Whether that something is external, internal, or neither is a question she stopped trying to solve.

Because the answers keep coming regardless.
