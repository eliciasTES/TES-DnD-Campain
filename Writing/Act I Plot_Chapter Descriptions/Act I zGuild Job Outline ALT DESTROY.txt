# Chapter 1 - Silence Before Blood

## 1. The Contract

Astrid presents the contract.

The client does not want the rival organization weakened.

The client wants it erased.

Objectives:

- Infiltrate the compound.
- Locate and extract the Brotherhood's informant.
- Leave no witnesses if possible.
- Make the attack unmistakably the work of the Dark Brotherhood.

> "When they speak of this place, I want them whispering."

---

## 2. Approach

Elias travels light.

He carries only:

- Sword
- Dagger
- Light armor
- A few potions

He avoids unnecessary tools.

His greatest weapons are stealth, magic, and speed.

---

## 3. Infiltration

After nightfall Elias silently enters the compound.

He slips past patrols.

Locks become meaningless through skill and magic.

He avoids combat until reaching the informant.

No alarms.

No suspicion.

---

## 4. Find the Informant

The informant is found alive inside the warehouse.

After a brief exchange Elias escorts them toward the planned escape route.

Only once the informant reaches safety does the second phase begin.

---

# Chapter 2 - Crimson Route

## 5. The Black Sacrament

Elias begins the assassination.

The objective changes from stealth...

...to terror.

Guards disappear one by one.

Lookouts fall from the watchtower.

Officers are slain before giving orders.

Panic spreads faster than any alarm.

---

## 6. Unleash Chaos

Elias turns the compound itself into a weapon.

He:

- Ignites lamp oil stores.
- Shatters support beams with destructive magic.
- Brings cranes crashing into warehouses.
- Releases frightened horses into the yard.
- Collapses stacked cargo onto fleeing guards.
- Sets barges ablaze along the docks.

The compound descends into complete chaos.

---

## 7. The Final Message

The organization's leader attempts to flee.

Elias intercepts them.

The leader is executed publicly.

Their body is left where every survivor will find it.

A Black Hand insignia is placed nearby.

There is no doubt who carried out the contract.

---

## 8. Escape

With the compound burning behind him, Elias disappears into the surrounding forest.

By the time local guards arrive:

- The warehouse is destroyed.
- Most of the leadership is dead.
- Survivors refuse to speak above a whisper.

---

## 9. Return

Astrid listens without emotion.

The informant is safe.

The contract is fulfilled.

The rival organization no longer exists.

Word spreads quickly across Skyrim.

Merchants refuse to do business with anyone connected to the destroyed company.

No one remembers what merchandise they traded.

Everyone remembers what happened to them.

The lesson is simple:

Cross the Dark Brotherhood...

...and there may be no one left to bury you.
