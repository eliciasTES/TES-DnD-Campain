## Chapter 1

Brynjolf sends Elias to investigate a rival organization stealing Guild smuggling routes.

Because nobody recognizes Elias, he can infiltrate the operation without exposing the Guild.

During the investigation, Elias discovers the missing Guild informant.

The mission suddenly becomes much larger than expected.

---

## Chapter 2

Elias must decide whether the informant is:

- Loyal
- A traitor
- Being coerced

He rescues (or confronts) the informant, sabotages the rival headquarters so it appears to be an accident, escapes, and returns to the Guild. This prevents the leader/financer of the organization to reconsider if competing with the guild is worth the trouble and investment again.

Brynjolf realizes Elias has proven himself.

He arranges Elias' long-awaited meeting with Maven Black-Briar.

The reward isn't gold.

It's **information**—the location of Virel.
