# Operation: Broken Route

## Mission Objective

Determine who is stealing Guild smuggling routes, identify the organization behind it, and cripple their operation without exposing the Thieves Guild.

---

# Brynjolf's Plan

## Phase 1 — Reconnaissance

- Travel to the suspected smuggling warehouse on Lake Geir.
- Confirm whether the rival organization is operating from the location.
- Avoid detection.

---

## Phase 2 — Infiltration

- Enter the headquarters after nightfall.
- Search for ledgers, maps, shipping manifests, and correspondence.
- Identify the organization's leader and primary financier.
- Leave everything appearing undisturbed.

---

## Phase 3 — Confirm the Informant's Fate

If the missing Guild informant is found:

- Determine whether they are:
  - Loyal
  - A traitor
  - Acting under coercion
- Bring them back alive if possible.
- If betrayal is confirmed beyond doubt, Brynjolf authorizes Elias to deal with them as he sees fit.

---

## Phase 4 — Sabotage

Before leaving:

- Destroy the organization's merchandise.
- Disable their warehouse and smuggling equipment.
- Leave evidence suggesting an accidental fire, structural collapse, or alchemical explosion.
- Avoid leaving evidence linking the Guild to the attack.

---

## Phase 5 — Escape

- Exit before guards organize.
- Use predetermined escape routes through the marshes or waterways.
- Return directly to the Ragged Flagon.

---

# Rules

- The Guild must remain anonymous.
- Avoid unnecessary killing.
- Recover information before destroying anything.
- The Guild wants the operation crippled—not a public gang war.
- If the mission goes wrong, Elias is on his own.

---

# Expected Outcome

- Rival smuggling operation destroyed.
- Guild routes secured.
- Missing informant accounted for.
- Rival investors discouraged from funding future competition.
- Elias earns Brynjolf's trust and a meeting with Maven Black-Briar.
