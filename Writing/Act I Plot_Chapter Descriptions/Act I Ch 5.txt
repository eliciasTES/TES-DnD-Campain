Elias Venym is a dark-skinned Imperial spellsword and wandering Daedra hunter whose life is defined by the search for truth rather than unquestioning faith. Raised as an ordinary citizen of the Empire, he joined the Vigilants of Stendarr believing that good and evil were simple opposites. While operating undercover inside a cult devoted to Namira, he discovered that many of its followers were ordinary people driven there by rejection, poverty, grief, and suffering rather than malice. After exposing the cult's corrupt leadership while sparing innocent followers, Elias became disillusioned with the Vigilants' rigid worldview and left the order.

Virel is a Dunmer shadowblade and former Tribunal acolyte whose life was shattered by a forbidden ritual that left her spiritually scarred and exiled from everything she once believed. Once a devoted servant of the Tribunal Temple, she became the victim of corruption hidden within its ranks, leaving her with fragmented memories, unstable shadow magic, and a deep uncertainty about her own identity. Though many consider her cursed, Virel refuses to let her past define her.

Elias Venym and Virel later travel Skyrim as wandering exiles with no permanent home, no allegiance to any kingdom or religious order, and no desire for fame. Though they come from very different backgrounds, both have been betrayed by the institutions they once trusted and now rely only on one another.

Elias gives Virel the secret book he store from the cult (the reason they pursue him with such intensity). He doesn't know the extent of its power or what secrets it holds but if he could not keep and protect it himself, he trusts her with the book more than the cultists. 

After an encounter in Helgen, killing bandits and freeing the captured survivors of the Dragon attack, Elias is finally in the Rift. He is prepared to get information about Virel or her whereabouts. When he gets the city of Riften and cannot get any good leads on her location ends up requesting the help of the Thieves Guild.



Elias has all the gear he needs and a traveling horse to carry his more cumbersome items. 

On his horse he keeps-

His large travel pack with a bedroll, cooking gear including an iron pot, bowl, and utensils, dried meat, bread, and preserved fruit, an extra large waterskin. The makeshift bow and arrows for emergency hunting, a spare bowstring, alchemy supplies including, ingredient bundles, mortar and pestle, empty bottles, and extra potions, a coil of rope, several hunter's snares, a fishing line, a thick blanket, and a hagraven book called "Darkside Utterings" filled with grotesque body manipulation alteration spells.

The gear he keeps on him include- 

A book written by hagravens who rule the forsworn of the reach called "Grimoire of the Briar Mothers" including spells and rituals of the Reach and the process of crafting Briar-hearts. Elias also has Anises journal documenting her travels across Tamriel and her journey to becoming an evil witch. The book has a torn out pages of her powerful fire destruction spells stuck in the back.

A mossy green and brown cloak Anise had gifted him, a small waterskin, a pouch of silver and gold coins and a small healing potion on his belt, a few rare ingredients, and finally his flint and steel.

His sword, a one-handed hardened steel arming sword with a weathered, battle-worn finish. Thin silver runes are etched along the fuller, while the simple blackened iron crossguard, worn black leather grip, and polished raven-bone pommel give it a practical yet ominous appearance. The blade is lean, well-balanced, and built for a spellsword, emitting a faint magical hum that hints at a powerful enchantment. It hums with an enchantment but Elias is not sure what it does. 




We open the chapter with Elias camping in the woods along a road on the south end of Lake Geir not far from Ivarstead. He is asleep and having a strange dream about Virel, the Return of the dragons and Namira. He has been hearing a voice he assumes to be Namira more and more frequently as she gets closer to Riften.
