

# Locations

## Ragged Flagon 1


The Ragged Flagon lay hidden beneath Riften like the beating heart of a forgotten city. Ancient stone vaults arched overhead, their age betrayed by moss creeping between weathered bricks and roots that pierced the ceiling in search of water. Lanterns and braziers cast pools of amber light across the cavern, softening the rough stone with the warmth of polished timber and worn oak furnishings.

Unlike the city's crowded inns, the Flagon possessed a quiet confidence. Thick wooden galleries and lattice partitions divided the common room into secluded corners where whispered deals could be struck without fear of eavesdroppers. The scent of roasting meat mingled with damp earth, pipe smoke, old ale, and the faint sweetness of moss growing wherever sunlight had once found a crack.

Beyond the tavern stretched a broad underground reservoir. A shallow pool reflected the lantern light like black glass, crossed by sturdy wooden bridges leading toward the Guild's workshops and hidden passages. Water trickled endlessly through ancient channels carved by forgotten masons, filling the chamber with a constant murmur that swallowed careless conversation. Ferns, ivy, and pale cave flowers clung to the stone walls, thriving in the perpetual moisture and lending the place an unexpected beauty.

The headquarters bore little resemblance to the lawless dens one might expect of thieves. It was lived in. Armor rested beside chairs worn smooth by decades of use. Books, maps, lockboxes, and ledgers shared tables with mugs and dice. Every beam had been repaired, every lantern intentionally placed, every hidden door maintained by countless unseen hands. It was less a tavern than an underground village—a sanctuary for those who survived by wit instead of honor.



## Ragged Flagon 2

The Ragged Flagon is a hidden underground tavern and headquarters built within an ancient stone cistern beneath Riften. Towering vaulted brick ceilings arch over weathered stone floors, crossed by sturdy timber bridges and raised wooden galleries overlooking a shallow, crystal-clear reservoir fed by trickling streams. Warm lanterns, braziers, and candlelight cast a golden glow that contrasts with the cool dampness of the cavern. Moss, ivy, hanging roots, and patches of cave ferns soften the aged stone, giving the forgotten structure a strangely natural beauty. Heavy oak tables, carved railings, secluded booths, shelves of bottles, and well-worn furniture fill the common room, while hidden passages, workshops, and private chambers branch deeper into the Guild's domain. The air carries the scent of roasted meat, ale, pipe smoke, damp earth, aged wood, and fresh water. Despite being the headquarters of the Thieves Guild, it feels less like a criminal hideout and more like a secret underground lodge that has been inhabited, repaired, and expanded over generations.	

## Ragged Flagon 3

The Ragged Flagon is a hidden underground tavern and headquarters beneath Riften, built within an ancient stone cistern. Warm lantern light reflects across shallow pools crossed by sturdy wooden bridges, while weathered timber galleries, moss-covered brickwork, and creeping ivy soften the cavern's age. The air carries the scent of ale, smoke, damp earth, and roasting food. Far more than a thieves' den, it is a well-kept sanctuary where merchants, rogues, smugglers, and Guild members gather beneath the city in quiet safety.
